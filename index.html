Projects

X-finity Mobile (Rise Course)

Client-Centric Mindset (Storyline Module)

This course was developed for all employees to explain how to keep client outcomes front of mind in all actions, and treating external clients and internal teams they serve with an appropriate level of professionalism.

Keeping Time (Game-based Activity)

This was part of a larger Rise course I developed to cover etiquette over different communication channels for Customer Service agents. My goal with this activity was to create an interactive way to make agents more aware of how long they keep customers on hold.

Producing a Paycheck (Rise Course)
